About Silver Wolf

    Silver Wolf is a playable character in Honkai: Star Rail with Quantum elemental skill
    attacks, she is a Nihility type character which that mainly deals to debuff enemies.
    She gives multiple stat debuffs from her passive and her skill add addtional weakness
    to the enemies, and her ultimate skill inflicts major defense debuff to an enemy.

    A member of the Stellaron Hunters and a genius hacker from a planet called "Punkelorde."
    Silver Wolf has mastered the skill known as "aether editing," which can be used to
    tamper with the data of reality. Hence, she always views the universe as a massive
    immersive simulation game and is eager to clear the stages awaiting ahead.

    Silver Wolf's Characteristics

  1. She forward to new challenges to beat.
  2. She can hack her way through almost any level.
  3. She treats the whole universe as a video game.
